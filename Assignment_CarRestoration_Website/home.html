About Jack Jetson

Hi! I'm Jack Jetson, and restoring classic Mini Coopers isn't just a hobby - it's my passion. There's nothing quite like bringing these iconic cars back to life, one bolt and brushstroke at a time. Whether you're here to appreciate the craftsmanship, get inspired, or maybe start your own restoration journey, take a look around. I'd love to share the stories behind these timeless classics and my process in making them shine again.
